Clinical Specializations

Support for a range of psychological and emotional concerns, with focus on early signs and timely help.

Relationship Issues

  • Frequent arguments
  • Trust issues or insecurity
  • Emotional distance
  • Break-up or separation stress

Depression / Stress

  • Low mood most days
  • Loss of interest
  • Sleep or appetite changes
  • Feeling tired or hopeless

Anxiety & Panic

  • Excessive worrying
  • Heart racing, breathlessness
  • Constant restlessness
  • Fear of losing control

OCD

  • Unwanted repetitive thoughts
  • Checking or cleaning rituals
  • Anxiety if rituals are stopped
  • Daily routine disturbed

Schizophrenia

  • Hearing voices
  • Confused thoughts
  • Social withdrawal
  • Unusual beliefs

Bipolar Disorder

  • Extreme mood shifts
  • High energy or irritability
  • Risky decisions
  • Low phases with sadness

Child Counselling

  • Behaviour issues
  • Academic stress
  • Emotional struggles
  • Fear or sleep issues

Career Counselling

  • Confusion about career choices
  • Lack of motivation
  • Difficulty planning future
  • Work stress

Personality Disorders

  • Unstable relationships
  • Fear of abandonment
  • Strong emotional shifts
  • Impulsive behaviour

Phobias

  • Intense specific fears
  • Avoiding places or activities
  • Panic when exposed
  • Daily life affected

Anger Issues

  • Frequent anger outbursts
  • Difficulty calming down
  • Irritability
  • Relationship strain

Sexual Problems

  • Low desire or performance stress
  • Fear or avoidance of intimacy
  • Shame or anxiety
  • Relationship difficulties

Drug De-addiction

  • Increasing use of substances
  • Cravings
  • Withdrawal symptoms
  • Loss of control

Migraine & Stress Headaches

  • Recurrent headaches
  • Sensitivity to light/sound
  • Stress-related triggers
  • Sleep disturbance

Trauma / PTSD

  • Nightmares or intrusive memories
  • Avoiding reminders
  • Feeling numb or detached
  • Hypervigilance

Grief & Loss

  • Persistent sadness
  • Difficulty accepting loss
  • Loss of interest
  • Feeling stuck emotionally
